Find answers to common questions about bike rentals and usage.

Rental fees depend on the bike type and the duration of use. For instance:

  • Mountain bikes: 10php per hour
  • Road bikes: 10php per hour
  • Electric bikes: 30php per hour

We offer a variety of bikes for rent:

  • Mountain Bikes: Perfect for off-road trails and rough terrain.
  • Road Bikes: Lightweight bikes ideal for long-distance, paved roads.
  • Electric Bikes: E-bikes with a motor assist for easier and faster rides.
